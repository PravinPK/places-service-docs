# Manage libraries

## Rename a library

1. Log in to Adobe Places with your Adobe ID.
2. Click the **Library** tab.
3. Click the edit \(pencil\) icon.
4. Enter the new name.
5. Click **Save**.

## Delete a library

1. Log in to Adobe Places with your Adobe ID.
2. Click the **Library** tab.
3. Click the delete \(trash can\) icon.
4. Click **Confirm**.
